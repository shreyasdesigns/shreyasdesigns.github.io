Document Viewer

Enterprise • BIM • Large-scale workflows

Overview

Designing a multi-dimensional viewer that integrates 2D drawings, models, and reality capture into a unified workflow.

The challenge was handling scale while maintaining clarity across document relationships, versioning, and review workflows.

Research

Conducted usability testing and workflow analysis across enterprise users to identify bottlenecks in document navigation and collaboration.

Insights revealed fragmentation between tools and lack of context while reviewing documents.
